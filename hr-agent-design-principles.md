# HR Agent Design Principles

These are the principles I think matter most when using agent workflows in HR.

## 1. Human-in-the-loop
Sensitive HR decisions should not run without human review.

## 2. Auditability
A workflow should leave a readable trail of what happened, what it used, and why it acted.

## 3. Privacy awareness
HR agent workflows should minimize exposure of sensitive employee and candidate data.

## 4. Bounded scope
A narrow useful workflow is better than a broad unreliable one.

## 5. Useful before impressive
The goal is not to look futuristic. The goal is to support real HR work responsibly.
